Novita AI GPUs provides:
- GPU Instance: A dedicated virtual machine with full control over GPU hardware, ideal for long-running, predictable workloads.
- Serverless GPUs: A fully managed, on-demand GPU service that dynamically scales and charges based on actual usage, perfect for burstable and sporadic tasks.
