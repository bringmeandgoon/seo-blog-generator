## ​Preliminary Preparations
Before deploying a new GPU instance, please be aware of the following points:
- First, create your Novita AI account and ensure you have enough credits in your account. Refer to our Quickstart for more information.
- Confirm that you have read and understood the product introductions, pricing, features, usage restrictions, and terms of service for Novita AI.
- Understand the billing methods for GPU instances. Here’s some guidance: GPU Instance Pricing.
